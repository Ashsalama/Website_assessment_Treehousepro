The process

We will start by determining which trees are suitable for holding a treehouse, and then
look at some sample plans to get an idea of what is possible and indicative costs. Next
we will work out the size, layout and style of the treehouse and come up with some
draft plans. After deciding on the plan to proceed with, we contract an engineer to go
through the design and ensure that it will be safe and stable, now and as the tree
grows. Once the plans are finalised, I will organise any necessary building consent and
construction can begin.



The building process starts with the platform, and after this has been constructed rigorous
testing will take place to ensure that it meets the highest standards. Next we will add the
wall framing andÂ  install the roof before building the floors, walls, entrances, windows and
railings. If your treehouse has a particular theme, this is where we will add the little touches
that bring that to life, such as doorknobs, lighting and decorative elements. Lastly comes
the really fun part as we add those special extras such as slides, bridges, fire poles, zip lines
and climbing nets to make your treehouse really stand out!
